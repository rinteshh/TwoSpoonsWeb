Our story

Two spoons,
one very happy kitchen

Two Spoons started the way all good things do — with two people, two spoons, and a shared refusal to settle for boring snacks. What began as weekend baking for friends is now a little kitchen turning out Korean cheese buns, gooey brownies and gifting bakes that people drive across town for.

We keep the batches small, the ingredients honest and the flavours a bit bold. Whether you're grabbing peri-peri fries after work or ordering a custom cake for a birthday — there's always a spoon (okay, two) with your name on it.
